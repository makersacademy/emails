Congratulations, we've reviewed your application and would like to invite you to interview at Makers Academy. The interview is fairly relaxed and will consist of three parts:

1. An introduction to Makers
2. A few logic games

Learning Materials

Before arriving you should have, at least, gone through at least the first 8 chapters of Chris Pine's 'Learn to Program' book.

You will also need to have fully completed Codecademy - Ruby.

You can also check out this resource for additional help: Lynda.com - Essentials of Ruby video course.

If you manage to do all three, even better!

How to Prep for the interview

  1. Book your interview, please follow this link: Book Your Interview. Set yourself a target date to get through the work. Joining Makers Academy requires you to be committed to code - this is the first step. You will need your Codecademy profile link for this step.
  2. Make sure you can access a dev environment. The easiest way to do this is to use C9. Alternatively, if you have a Mac, why not set up your own? Follow the step-by-step tutorial here. (don't use the script option at the top - you won't learn anything!)
  3. You'll need to work through Chris Pine's tutorial on your laptop and do each exercise. Please note - reading books and watching videos just won't count - when it comes to coding, you have to learn by doing. This is why it's essential that you do the exercises in the book.
  4. Keep to a schedule to study, ie: 2 hours every evening. Don't cram for the interview the day before - we can tell.
  5. Make sure you have gone through the required materials, had a stab at the exercises, and digested the content. We're more interested in how you learn to code that how much you can code.
  6. Do your research. We get worried if people don't have questions about learning to code or joining Makers!
  7. We advise you to allow 7 - 10 days of study to prepare for the interview and book your interview accordingly. You can book a Skype interview, but we prefer you to come into Makers Academy in person.

If you can't find a time to suit you please get in touch with us ASAP and we look forward to meeting you soon.

All the best and good luck!
